# Code Of Conduct

CrabiX community spaces are for practical, respectful discussion about AI tooling, local-first workflows, developer productivity, and the CrabiX product.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and specific.
- Assume good intent, but keep feedback concrete.
- Critique ideas and implementation, not people.
- Share reproducible details when reporting bugs.
- Respect privacy, security boundaries, and disclosure rules.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Hate speech or discriminatory language.
- Posting private information without consent.
- Publishing secrets, credentials, exploit details, or vulnerability reports in public channels.
- Spam or misleading promotion.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may hide, edit, or remove content and may restrict participation when behavior harms the project or community.

For urgent concerns, contact [support@crabix.ai](mailto:support@crabix.ai).
